Our Services

DLF offers a wide range of logistics services, including sea-freight, air-freight, customs clearance, and warehousing. With a focus on dangerous goods handling and a dedicated fleet, they ensure efficient transport and delivery solutions.

Sea-Freight Services

DLF offers a comprehensive range of sea-freight solutions. Whether you need Full Container Load (FCL), Less than Container Load (LCL), reefer cargo, or project cargo services, we have you covered. Our expertise ensures your cargo is handled with the utmost care and efficiency.

  • FCL Services (Door-Door)
  • Reefer Cargo Services (Door-Door)
  • General Consolidation and LCL Services (Door-Door)
  • Multimodal & Intermodal Services (Door-Door)
  • Project and Break Bulk Cargo Logistics
  • Dangerous Goods/HAZ ISO Cargo Handling

Air-Freight Services

Experience the versatility of our air-freight services. From general cargo to special cargo that includes dangerous goods and perishables, we provide solutions that cater to your specific needs. Additionally, we offer aircraft chartering services to accommodate your unique requirements.

  • General Cargo Services
  • Regular Economical Services
  • Special Cargo Services (Dangerous Goods, Perishable, HAZ Samples)
  • Aircraft Chartering Services

Customs Clearance Services

Our proficiency in customs clearance sets us apart. We excel in import and export clearance, duty drawback application, and expert consultation. Our pre-clearance services streamline the process, while our comprehensive knowledge ensures compliance with regulations.

  • In-house Import and Export Clearance
  • Duty Drawback Application
  • Consulting and Advice
  • Pre-Shipment Authorisation and Pre-Clearance
  • Export Document Preparation
  • Customs Audit Assistance
  • Tariff Classification

Warehousing, Transport & Logistics Services

DLF takes an integrated approach to logistics. With our dedicated trucking fleet, specialized container transport, and container packing/unpacking capabilities, your cargo is in safe hands. We also specialize in bulk liquid transport and nationwide delivery, ensuring efficient and reliable solutions for all your logistical needs.

  • DGL Owned Trucking Fleet and Warehouses
  • Container Transport (Sideloaders, A Doubles, B Doubles, Flat Decks)
  • Specialised Container Transport (Dropdeck Sideloader, Light Skel Sideloader)
  • Bulk Liquid Transport (HAZ/Non HAZ)
  • Container Packing and Unpacking Warehouse
  • Storage (DG and General Cargo)
  • Nationwide Delivery (DG and General Cargo)
